** Setup

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(defun mcg--setup-vertico ()
  "Setup vertico."
  (when (mcg-require-extension "vertico" t)
    (vertico-mode 1)
    (mcg-log "vertico configured")))
#+end_src

** Initialize

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
